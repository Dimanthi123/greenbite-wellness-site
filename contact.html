Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I update my meal plan?
We recommend updating your meal plan every 4-6 weeks to keep your meals interesting and ensure nutritional variety. However, if your goals change or you feel stuck in a rut, feel free to update it more frequently.
Can I use GreenBite if I have dietary restrictions?
Absolutely! Our recipe filters allow you to select options that accommodate various dietary needs including gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, and vegan preferences. You can also contact us for personalized advice.
How accurate is the calorie calculator?
Our calculator uses widely accepted formulas for estimating BMR and TDEE. However, individual metabolism can vary, so consider these numbers as a starting point and adjust based on your personal results and needs.
Do I need equipment for the workouts?
Many of our workouts require no equipment at all. You can filter workouts based on the equipment you have available, whether it's just your bodyweight, dumbbells, resistance bands, or other fitness accessories.
